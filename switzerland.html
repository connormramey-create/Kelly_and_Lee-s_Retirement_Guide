Switzerland

You know it, you love it, it's an info dump people!

The capital of Switzerland is Bern known for it's well perserved "Old Town". Some of Switzerland's main exports are Pharmaceuticals & Chemicals, Precious Metals & Stones (especially gold refining/transit) and Watches & Clocks. The currency of Switzerland is the Swiss Franc. The main three languages in Switzerland are German, French, and Italian, in that order so study up!

Switzerland is one of the most well known European destinations for beautiful winter views. See the tops of glaciers and even get to see all the cool stuff Howard talks about!
